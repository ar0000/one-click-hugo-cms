Bangladesh is at an exciting inflection point in its infrastructure journey. As the country pushes toward its Vision 2041 development goals, investment in roads, bridges, urban infrastructure, and energy systems is accelerating at an unprecedented pace.

## The Scale of the Challenge

Rapid urbanization, a growing population, and climate vulnerability combine to create both urgency and complexity in infrastructure planning. Poor road connectivity in rural areas limits agricultural productivity and market access. Urban flooding and inadequate drainage systems impose billions of dollars in annual economic losses.
